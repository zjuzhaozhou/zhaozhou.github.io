Description

Users have been facing increasing challenges on efficiently understanding video content, as the amount of video content is growing explosively. Video summarisation aims to address this issue by distilling the essence of a video to produce a visual summary and it has been a key topic of multimedia research. Recent years various deep learning techniques have been developed for video summarization, owing to the great success of deep learning in various tasks of analysing visual content. Meanwhile, various conventional approaches have also achieved great success along the line of less data intensive way. Therefore, this tutorial aims to provide the audience in the multimedia community an integrated view of both conventional approaches and deep learning based approaches. Future development along video summarization will also be discussed to stimulate stronger interests in video summarization for a broader range of applications of video summarization.
